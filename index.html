Welcome to Denver, a city with something for everyone, and a fantastic place to visit. But not live. As locals will tell you, jokingly serious, we are full!

The Many Nicknames of Denver

  • The Mile High City (due to an elevation of 5280 feet above sea level)
  • Queen City of the Plains
  • Queen City of the West
  • Broncoville
  • Wall Street of the West
  • Cow Town

Every year, Denver still reaches back to the agricultural roots that earned it the nickname "Cow Town." An large annual event, the National Western Stock Show, traditionally ends with the Grand Champion Steer visiting the historical Brown Palace Hotel for tea and a photo op. Yes, this is the best cow from the stock show in a fancy hotel lobby having its picture taken with all the people who have come to see him. This has been Denver a tradition for over 76 years.
